About Us

We Are Increasing Business Success With Technology

Our journey started through our passion for providing IT consulting and software  development services. Under the prestigious leadership of Muhammed Shibily, Muhammed  Koya, and Ashmi Jamal, EssbeeTechstrategies Private Limited in March 2021 flourished and  grew bigger as the days passed. Our head office is based in Kerala, India, and we look  forward to expanding further into other states in India. Global expansion is one of the key  objectives that our board members have been consistent with. 
Being independent and well organized, this company takes an agile and radical approach to custom software development outsourcing. We are determined to provide quality services  benefits in improving your business performance and increasing your reach in a fashion never  witnessed by you prior to this.
Your satisfaction becomes the ultimate goal for us. We are proficient in understanding the  business challenges and competence, which further allows us to enhance the customer experience. Knowledge sees no end, and we ensure that our team continues learning by  adapting to the perpetual market requirements and the modern technologies that could be implemented to bring forth a drastic change in your business portfolio. Therefore, we ensure our proficiency in a few of the basics and the advanced technologies such as Python, .NET,  Java, PHP, JavaScript, and C++ to help companies adopt multiple new technologies.
Being a non-government company, we have been registered with the Ministry of Corporate  Affairs (MCA), under Registrar of Companies Cochin Kerala India.
